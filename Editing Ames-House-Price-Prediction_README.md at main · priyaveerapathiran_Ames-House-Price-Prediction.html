Ames-House-Price-Prediction

Introduction

Estimating the value of real estate is a difficult one. There are various factors that impacts the price of price of house like House Condition, Demographis, Interest rate, Real estate price etc. Here I have considered the area, condition, year built, remodeling, number of rooms, bathroom etc. to predict the house pricing. In this study did Exploratory ddata analysis and used Linear Regression to predict the house pricing. The Ames Housing dataset was compiled by Dean De Cock for use in data science education.

This dataset contains 79 explanatory variables describing (almost) every aspect of residential homes in Ames, Iowa, this competition challenges you to predict the final price of each home.

Goal
To identify what are the features plays a key role in house pricing

Steps by step Process
1. I used Ames dataset from kaggle
2. Started with preprocessing of the data
3. Followed by Exploratory Data Analysis
4. Visualization on the house price data
5. Model evalution - Linear Regression
